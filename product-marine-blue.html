MARINE BLUE

Marine Blue is the premium plywood for shipbuilding, cabinetry, and much more. Crafted from tropical wood and featuring a 100% composed veneer, Marine Blue offers superior strength and durability. With 0.30 mm face veneer thickness and higher standard emission norms, it’s perfect for both indoor and outdoor furniture. Marine Blue is fire-safe, anti-bacterial, and resistant to pests, with a 25-year warranty. It remains straight even in high-pressure impact conditions, making it a reliable choice for any project.
