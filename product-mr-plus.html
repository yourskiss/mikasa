MR+

MR+ is a high-quality moisture-resistant plywood, perfect for upscale packaging, furniture parts, and more.
It is made from tropical wood with a face veneer thickness of 0.30mm. This grade is crafted to meet higher emission standards and is resistant to borers, fermitas, and microbial attacks.
The machine composed veneer is perfectly calibrated, bonded with El certified resin. Plus, with o 15-year warranty, MR+ is the ply for long-lasting durability.
